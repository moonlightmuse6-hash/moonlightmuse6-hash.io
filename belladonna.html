Deadly Nightshade (Belladonna): Beauty with a Bite

With glossy black berries and delicate purple blooms, belladonna (Atropa belladonna) looks innocent—but don’t be fooled. This infamous plant packs lethal tropane alkaloids, including atropine, which can paralyze the nervous system in just a few berries. Symptoms strike fast: blurred vision, racing heart, confusion, and convulsions. Historically it was used in potions and poison. Admire its gothic charm from afar, because this "beautiful lady" (as its name means) is anything but kind.
