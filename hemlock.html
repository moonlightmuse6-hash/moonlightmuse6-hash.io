Bleeding Heart: A Romantic Risk

With delicate white umbels resembling Queen Anne’s lace, hemlock (Conium maculatum) hides its deadliness in plain sight. This infamous killer,responsible for Socrates’ execution, contains coniin, a neurotoxin that paralyzes muscles, including those needed for breathing. Just a few leaves can trigger violent tremors, vomiting, paralysis, and death by respiratory failure. Found in ditches and meadows, it’s easily mistaken for edible herbs like wild carrot but there’s no second chance with this plant. Handle? Never. Taste? Fatally foolish.
