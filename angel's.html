Angel’s Trumpet: A Deadly Beauty

Angel’s trumpet (Brugmansia) dazzles with its hanging, fragrant blooms, but every part of this plant contains deadly toxins. Its scopolamine and atropine can cause hallucinations, paralysis, and even fatal respiratory failure if ingested. Symptoms appear quickly: dilated pupils, dry mouth, rapid pulse, confusion, and muscle weakness. Just touching it may cause numbness. Despite its beauty, this plant demands extreme caution—admire it, but do not touch or taste!
